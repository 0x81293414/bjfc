About Berkley FC

Our History

Founded in 2011, Berkley FC has grown to become one of Liverpool's leading grassroots football clubs, dedicated to developing young talent.

Our Mission

We aim to provide high-quality football education while fostering teamwork, respect, and personal development in every player.

Our Values

Excellence, inclusivity, and community spirit are at the heart of everything we do, creating a positive environment for players to thrive.
